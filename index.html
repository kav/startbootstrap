About

Learn how to see?

Eyes on Track helps professionals, educators and parent's understand the nature of near vision problems and their effects on learning and provides therapeutic techniques to be used in the classroom. It is full of illustrations and fun games and activities that can be duplicated for multiple uses, some designed for individual use or for the classroom or small groups.
